Welcome to The Zen POS

The zen pos is simple point of sales made with angularjs + mobileangularui for the sake of showing the world that web app could be in everywhere right now we got webapp-version and phonegap-version we plan build cloud-version in the future

Quick Start guide

$ copy thezenpos to your www folder
$ try it with http://localhost/thezenpos in your browser
$ done! you can use thezenpos now
